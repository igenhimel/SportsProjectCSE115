                                               **Man of the Match in the Final**

There were no Man of the Tournament awards before 1992 but Man of the Match awards have 
always been given for individual matches. Winning the Man of the Match in the final is 
logically noteworthy, as this indicates the player deemed to have played the biggest 
part in the World Cup final. To date the award has always gone to a member of the winning side.
